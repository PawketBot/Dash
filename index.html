Privacy Policy

5. What Nova Can Access

Nova receives data from Discord to operate correctly, including:

  • Message content
  • Usernames and user IDs

None of this data is stored, except as outlined below.

6. What We Store

Nova stores:

This information is used only to provide and personalize Nova’s functionality on a per-server basis.
